Our Journey

From a garage in Bengaluru to the skies of India

SkyArc began with a simple observation: the drone industry was importing solutions that weren't built for India's extreme topography and atmospheric unpredictability.

Our founders, a group of aerospace engineers and data scientists, dedicated three years to building a flight controller from the ground up—one that could handle everything from the thin air of the Himalayas to the heavy humidity of the Western Ghats.

Today, we aren't just building drones; we are building the neural network of Indian aerospace.

Built year by year

2021

The Spark

Initial R&D phase in a shared workspace in Indiranagar. First autonomous hover achieved with the "Argus V1" prototype.

2023

Fleet Deployment

Launch of the SkyArc Fleet Management platform. Secured strategic partnerships with three state governments for medical delivery.

2024

Precision Horizon

Integration of AI-driven terrain following and 5G connectivity across the entire drone ecosystem.

2026

The Galactic Jump

Projected launch of the first low-orbit monitoring satellite constellation designed for real-time drone routing.
